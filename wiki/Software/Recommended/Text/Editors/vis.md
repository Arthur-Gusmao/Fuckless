## vis

A modern, minimal, and scriptable text editor inspired by Vim, Sam, and Acme.

vis keeps the modal editing model popularized by Vim while avoiding decades of accumulated complexity.

Rather than extending Vim's design indefinitely, vis rethinks it with a simpler architecture and a smaller, more coherent feature set.

### Simplicity

Rather than extending Vim's design indefinitely, vis rethinks it with a simpler architecture and a smaller, more coherent feature set.

For a project focused on doing one thing well, this is a significant advantage.

### Better Design

vis borrows ideas from Plan 9 editors such as Sam and Acme.

Selections are first-class objects instead of an afterthought, making many editing operations more natural and composable.

## dvtm

A dynamic virtual terminal manager inspired by dwm.

dvtm brings the ideas of dynamic tiling window management to the terminal.

Instead of manually arranging panes, layouts are managed automatically, allowing users to focus on their work rather than window management.

dvtm applies the same philosophy that made dwm successful: a small codebase, predictable behavior, and a focus on doing one thing well.

It offers the essential features of a terminal multiplexer without the complexity of larger alternatives.
